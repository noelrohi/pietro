## App Store requirements

Must include:
- Restore purchases link (top corner)
- Terms of Use link
- Privacy Policy link
- Clear pricing with billing period
- Cancel/refund information

## Timing

- Pre-paywall value: 30-60 seconds
- Paywall screen: User-paced (no auto-advance)
- Total time before paywall: 15-20 minutes

## Conversion optimization

### High-converting elements:
- Specific date/timeframe visible
- App preview showing personalized content
- Social proof (user count, ratings)
- Testimonials with specific results
- Savings percentage on yearly
- Risk reversal guarantee

### Low-converting elements:
- Feature lists without emotional benefit
- Generic stock photos
- Vague promises ("get fit", "be productive")
- Price shown without context
- Missing social proof
- No risk reversal

## Pitfalls

- Paywall appears before sufficient investment
- Features listed without benefits
- No price anchoring (single option)
- Missing risk reversal
- CTA uses transactional language
- Social proof absent from paywall
- Yearly savings not prominent enough
